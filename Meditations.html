Preparation

Find a quiet place. Put on comfortable clothes. Light some candles if you wish. Have your journal ready.

Breathing

Close your eyes. Take five slow, deep breaths. Inhale for 5 counts, hold for 5 counts, exhale slowly.

Elevator Descent

Imagine entering an elevator. Press the button to descend seven floors into your consciousness.

Garden Visualization

You're in a beautiful sacred garden. Notice the trees, flowers, and birds. What color is the sky?

Sensory Experience

Feel the earth beneath your feet. Is it grassy or sandy? Notice the temperature and wind.

Sacred Seat

Create a sacred meditation seat where you can find all the answers you seek.

Journal Prompts:

  • What am I most afraid of?
  • What aspects of my life need transforming?
  • What do I want to accomplish by reading this book?
  • What am I most afraid of that someone else will find out about me?
  • What am I most afraid of in finding out about myself?
  • What’s the biggest lie I’ve ever told myself?
  • What’s the biggest lie I’ve ever told someone else?
  • What could stop me from doing the work necessary to transform my life?

Preparation

Clear your space of distractions. Have your journal, crayons, and pen ready. Consider playing soft music.

Initial Breathing

Close your eyes and take a slow, deep breath. Use your breath to quiet your mind. Take five more slow, deep breaths.

Elevator Visualization

Imagine an elevator within you. Step in and descend seven floors. Step out into your beautiful garden.

Garden Experience

Walk through your garden. Notice the flowers and trees. Look at the lush green leaves and savor the rich smells. Listen to the birds singing.

Sacred Self Connection

Find your meditation seat. Close your eyes. An aspect of yourself - your sacred self - will come into your consciousness.

Sacred Self Interaction

Take your sacred self by the hand. Look it in the eye. Ask if it will guide and protect you this week.

Final Questions

Ask what you need to do to open your heart and let go of emotional toxicity. Embrace your sacred self and thank them.

Journal Prompts:

  • What did your sacred self look like?
  • What did your sacred self say to you?
  • How did you feel during the meditation?
  • What emotions or insights arose during the experience?
  • What do you need to do to open your heart and let go of emotional toxicity?

Preparation

Find a quiet space. Close your eyes and take five very slow, deep breaths. Inhale to the count of five, retain your breath as long as comfortable, then exhale slowly.

Elevator Descent

Imagine yourself going into an elevator and down seven floors. Use your breath to quiet your mind and go deep within your consciousness.

Dark Space Visualization

When you open the elevator door, you see a very dark and dingy place. Imagine the worst possible circumstances. Notice the smells, the filth, the garbage everywhere.

Shadow Encounter

Look down into a corner and see the lowest form of yourself imaginable. Allow an image of you at your worst to appear in your mind. Try to sense and see everything about this aspect.

Shadow Description

Allow a word that describes the person you are seeing to come into your mind. Stay with this image and this word.

Journal Prompts:

  • Describe the environment you encountered in detail.
  • What specific traits or qualities did you observe in your shadow self?
  • What emotions or physical sensations arose during the meditation?
  • What word came to describe this aspect of yourself?
  • What insights or understanding emerged from this encounter?

Initial Preparation

Find your sacred garden. Close your eyes and return to this safe space. Take deep breaths to quiet your mind.

Garden Entry

Take your internal elevator down seven floors and enter your garden. Walk through and admire its beauty.

Sacred Self Connection

Find your meditation seat. When comfortable, bring forth the image of your sacred self. Bask in their light and presence.

Shadow Emergence

Now, call forth the dark, shadowy aspect of yourself. Allow it to emerge fully in your awareness.

Integration Beginning

Ask your sacred self to approach and embrace your shadow self. Watch as they move toward each other.

Loving Embrace

Allow your sacred self to hold your shadow aspect. Send love, kindness, and forgiveness to your dark side.

Integration Message

Tell this dark aspect it is safe and that you will spend time understanding and learning to love it.

Journal Prompts:

  • How did it feel to witness the embrace between your sacred and shadow selves?
  • What resistance or acceptance did you experience?
  • What insights emerged about integration?
  • What emotions arose during this process?
  • What do you need to continue this integration work?
